FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does pool cleaning cost in Johannesburg?

It depends on your pool's size and condition, so we quote each pool individually — and quotes are always free. WhatsApp us a photo of your pool on +27 83 688 3238 and you'll usually have a price the same day, with no call-out fee.

Can you fix a green pool?

Yes — green-to-clean rescues are one of our specialities. Depending on how far gone the water is, most pools are sparkling again within a few days of treatment, not weeks.

Which areas do you service?

We're based in Johannesburg and service the city and surrounding areas. Message us with your location and we'll confirm availability in your area right away.

How often should a pool be serviced?

We recommend a weekly visit in swimming season and at least every second week in winter. Regular servicing keeps the chemistry balanced, prevents algae, and catches small equipment problems before they become expensive ones.

Do you repair pool pumps, filters and heaters?

Yes. We diagnose and repair pumps, filters, gas and electric heaters, and we do pressure testing to find hidden leaks. If a part is beyond saving, we'll quote you on a replacement before doing any work.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No — as long as our team can access the pool area, you don't need to be home. Many of our weekly clients simply arrange gate access and come home to a clean pool.
